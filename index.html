About SMATI

Discover our history, mission, and values

Our Story

St. Michael Arcangel Technological Institute, Inc. (SMATI) was established with a vision to provide quality education that empowers students to become competent professionals and responsible citizens.

For over a decade, we have been committed to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development, preparing our students for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities including modern computer laboratories, culinary arts kitchens, science laboratories, and a well-stocked library to support our students' learning journey.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops globally competitive and morally upright individuals.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for excellence in technological education.

Modern Classrooms

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with the latest technology to enhance the learning experience. Each room features:

  • Interactive smart boards
  • High-speed internet connectivity
  • Comfortable ergonomic furniture
  • Climate control systems
  • Audio-visual equipment

These facilities create an optimal environment for both traditional and digital learning methodologies.

Computer Laboratory

Computer Laboratory

Our state-of-the-art computer labs provide students with access to the latest hardware and software for their technical education.

  • High-performance desktop computers
  • Specialized software for programming, design, and analysis
  • 3D printers and scanning equipment
  • Dedicated servers for networking practice
  • 24/7 access for project work

These facilities support our IT, computer science, and engineering programs with hands-on learning opportunities.

Culinary Arts Kitchen

Culinary Arts Kitchen

Our professional-grade kitchen facilities provide students in our Hotel and Restaurant Management program with real-world culinary experience.

  • Commercial-grade ovens and stoves
  • Professional baking and pastry stations
  • Food safety and sanitation equipment
  • International cuisine preparation areas
  • Demonstration kitchen with viewing gallery

Students learn from experienced chefs and gain practical skills for careers in the food service industry.

Library

Library

Our comprehensive library provides students with access to a vast collection of resources to support their academic journey.

  • Over 50,000 physical books and journals
  • Digital access to academic databases
  • Quiet study areas and group collaboration spaces
  • Computer stations with research software
  • Professional librarians for research assistance

The library serves as a hub for academic research and independent study, supporting all programs offered at SMATI.

Science Laboratory

Science Laboratory

Our fully-equipped science laboratories provide students with hands-on experience in various scientific disciplines.

  • Chemistry lab with fume hoods and safety equipment
  • Biology lab with microscopes and specimen collections
  • Physics lab with measurement and experimentation tools
  • Environmental science testing equipment
  • Computerized data collection systems

These facilities support our STEM programs and provide practical experience that complements theoretical learning.

Gymnasium

Gymnasium

Our multi-purpose gymnasium serves as a venue for physical education, sports competitions, and campus events.

  • Full-size basketball and volleyball courts
  • Indoor track for running and walking
  • Fitness equipment and weight training area
  • Locker rooms with shower facilities
  • Seating capacity for 500 spectators

The gymnasium promotes physical wellness and serves as a gathering space for campus community events.

Auditorium

Auditorium

Our state-of-the-art auditorium is designed for academic presentations, performances, and large gatherings.

  • Seating capacity for 800 people
  • Professional sound and lighting systems
  • High-definition projection equipment
  • Stage with dressing rooms
  • Recording and broadcasting capabilities

The auditorium hosts guest lectures, graduation ceremonies, cultural performances, and other important campus events.

Student Lounge

Student Lounge

Our comfortable student lounge provides a relaxing environment for students to socialize, study, and recharge between classes.

  • Comfortable seating areas
  • Study nooks with charging stations
  • Refreshment area with vending machines
  • Board games and recreational activities
  • Student organization bulletin boards

The lounge serves as a central hub for student interaction and provides a welcoming space for the campus community.

Sports Facilities

Sports Facilities

Our outdoor sports facilities provide students with opportunities for athletic development and recreational activities.

  • Olympic-size swimming pool
  • Outdoor basketball and tennis courts
  • Soccer field with artificial turf
  • Track and field facilities
  • Outdoor fitness stations

These facilities support our physical education program and provide recreational opportunities for the entire campus community.

Cafeteria

Cafeteria

Our campus cafeteria offers nutritious meals and snacks in a comfortable dining environment.

  • Multiple food stations with diverse menu options
  • Healthy meal choices and dietary accommodations
  • Comfortable seating for 300 students
  • Outdoor patio dining area
  • Payment options including student meal plans

The cafeteria serves as a social hub where students can enjoy meals, study, and connect with peers in a relaxed setting.
